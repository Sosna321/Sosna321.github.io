Predictors of length of hospital stay for preterm infants in Ethiopia: A competing-risk analysis

Bonger, Z. T., Mamo, B. T., Birra, S. B., & Yalew, A. W. (2023). Frontiers in Pediatrics, 11, Article 1268087. https://doi.org/10.3389/fped.2023.1268087

This study applies a competing-risk model to identify predictors of hospital length of stay among preterm infants in Ethiopia, providing evidence to inform neonatal care and resource allocation.
